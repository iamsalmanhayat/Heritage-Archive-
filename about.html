Founded in 1907, the Organization of Pakistani Historians (OPH) is the largest professional society in Pakistan dedicated to the teaching and study of subcontinent history. The mission of the organization is to promote excellence in the scholarship, teaching, and presentation of Pakistani history, and to encourage wide discussion of historical questions and the equitable treatment of all practitioners of history.
